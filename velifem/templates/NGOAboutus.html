What We Do



Animal Rescue
There are so many animals day in & day out that are subjected to cruelty, road rage or just medical issues. The Velifem works towards helping such animals either on site or by admitting them into our facility.
Animal Adoption
Our team of Adoption volunteers work day in & day out finding good homes for the stray & abandoned animals. Every adoption happens after a strict screening & house visit by our approved volunteers..
Animal Birth Control (ABCs)
The Velifem truly believes that the best way to reduce animal – human conflict & animal – animal conflict is birth control. We conduct ABC (Animal Birth Control) surgeries once every week at our facility & keep the animal for Post Operative care until they are fit to be release back where they were picked up from.
Animal Health Care
The Velifem cares for the health of animals, involving all sorts of animals, in many different ways - from vaccines to food ad medicines.

Vision and Mission



Our Vision and Mission is to help, care and make all the animals those were abused, hurt, abandoned et cetra feel loved at all times no matter how hard the situation gets in today's world.

The VELIFEM is an NGO for stray animals to provide them with all the necesary health care and attention they deserve.
